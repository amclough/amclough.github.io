Dwelling.com

I recently onboarded as a Product Management contractor with Dwelling, a startup through the Harvard Innovation Lab. Dwelling is a home management system seeking to simplify repairs and home improvement. In addition to helping with the initial MVP's UI/UX design, I've been exercising my design muscles making promotional materials. Visit the site here, and check out some of my design work below!
